About Us

Welcome To Foodie Moodie

Little Story

We strive to showcase the diversity of local ingredients, taking into account local values, needs and seasonality. We want to evoke traditional flavors by rethinking their appearance a bit. We consider it important to be a place where the food does not have to be explained, but the guest still has a lasting experience from the first moment until departure.

Our Kitchen

We do not try to copy Grandma's kitchen, but we take out the old recipes, but we filter it through our own style, to such an extent that it is interesting for the contemporary public.
